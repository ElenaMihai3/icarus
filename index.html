Icarus

Daedalus, a master craftsman in the Greek history, helped Minos’ wife mate with a bull sent by Poseidon, but he also helped Ariadne, Mino’s daughter lover to find his way through the labyrinth to kill the minotaur, which was the son of the bull and the wife of Minos.
After these commited crimes, Minos imprisoned Daedalus and his son, Icarus, in a tower in a labyrinth on Kreta. Now Daedalus being the genius inventor he always was, decided to build wings from wax and feathers so he and his son could fly away.
